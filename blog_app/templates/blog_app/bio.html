About Tomozart

Thomas “Tomo” Overment is a multidisciplinary artist and developer based in Bath, UK, working under the name Tomozart. His work brings together traditional art, digital design, and modern technology to create a practice that sits between handcrafted expression and contemporary digital culture.

Drawing inspiration from nature, symbolism, mythology, movement, and urban visual language, Tomo creates artwork that aims to feel both raw and considered. His creative process moves across mediums, from drawing and painting to digital image-making, interactive web design, and creative technology.

Alongside his artistic practice, Tomo has trained in full-stack software engineering and data analytics with AI. This technical background shapes the way he builds and presents his work, allowing Tomozart to exist not only as an art brand, but as an evolving digital space where creativity, experimentation, and storytelling meet.

Tomozart is both a gallery and a working studio environment — a place to share original pieces, creative experiments, and selected works available as prints and products. Whether through one-off artworks, collectable designs, or future shop releases, the aim is to create pieces that people can connect with and bring into their own spaces.

At its core, Tomozart is about building something lasting: a body of work and a creative platform that blends art, identity, and innovation.
